Types of Home Insurance Ontario Coverage

Home insurance Ontario typically includes several types of coverage to protect your home and its contents:

1. Property Coverage

Property coverage protects the physical structure of your home, including walls, roof, and foundation, from damages caused by fires, storms, or vandalism.

2. Contents Coverage

Contents coverage protects your personal belongings inside the home, such as furniture, electronics, and clothing, against theft or damage.

3. Liability Coverage

This coverage helps protect you from financial loss if someone is injured on your property or if you accidentally damage another person’s property.

4. Additional Living Expenses

If your home becomes uninhabitable due to a covered loss, additional living expenses coverage helps pay for temporary housing and living expenses.
